Glow Up Diary — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Glow Up Diary (the "App") is developed and published by Abdul Ahad, operating as Undercover Mysteries ("we," "us," or "our"), based in Pakistan. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it's used, who it's shared with, and the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account information

To use the App, you create an account using either an email address and password, or Google Sign-In. Depending on the method you choose, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name
  • If you sign in with Google, your Google account's profile picture URL

Account authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication, a Google service. We do not see or store your password directly — Firebase manages this on our behalf.

1.2 App content you create

Your daily selfie photos are stored only on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers, Firebase, or any third party. Deleting the App or your account removes these files from your device.

The following information about your activity in the App is stored in our cloud database (Cloud Firestore, a Google service), tied to your account:

  • The date and day number of each selfie you take, and an on-device file reference — not the photo itself
  • Your daily check-in data: which habits you marked complete, an optional mood rating, your logged water intake, and any optional free-text note you choose to write
  • Any custom habits you create (name, icon, and color)
  • Your subscription/Pro status and the date your 90-day challenge started

Because the note field is free text, please avoid entering sensitive personal information you wouldn't want stored — whatever you type is saved as written.

1.3 Purchases and subscriptions

If you purchase a Pro subscription, the transaction itself is processed entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play — we never see or store your payment card details. To manage entitlements, we use RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service, which receives your app-assigned user ID and your purchase/subscription history. RevenueCat's own privacy practices are described at revenuecat.com/privacy.

1.4 Advertising

Free-tier use of the App includes ads served by Google AdMob. When an ad is shown, Google's AdMob SDK may collect your device's advertising identifier and ad interaction data, in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. We do not control what Google collects through this SDK beyond what we configure. You can read Google's practices at policies.google.com/privacy and, specifically for how Google uses data from apps using its services, at google.com/policies/privacy/partners.

1.5 Information that never leaves your device

Your selfie photo files, your light/dark theme preference, and your daily reminder time and on/off setting are all stored locally on your device only, using local storage mechanisms. Daily reminders are scheduled and delivered entirely on-device — no notification content is ever sent through a remote server.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account, and let you sign in across sessions
  • To save and sync your habit check-ins, mood logs, and selfie metadata so your progress is preserved
  • To verify and manage your Pro subscription status
  • To show ads to free-tier users, and to measure ad performance
  • To respond to support requests sent to our contact email
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the App

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your check-in notes or selfie metadata for advertising targeting.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and app data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account from within the App, your account record, check-ins, selfie metadata, and custom habits are permanently deleted from our database, and your locally stored photo files are deleted from your device. This action cannot be undone.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights including the ability to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise most of these directly in the App (editing your name, deleting individual selfies, or deleting your account entirely), or by contacting us using the details below.

If you are in the European Economic Area or UK, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), including the right to know what's collected and to request deletion. We do not sell personal information as defined under CCPA.

6. International Data Transfers

Our infrastructure providers (Google/Firebase) may process and store data on servers located outside your home country. By using the App, you understand your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws than your own.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

We rely on Firebase's built-in security infrastructure (including authentication and database access rules) to protect your data. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
