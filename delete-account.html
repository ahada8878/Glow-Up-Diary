Account Deletion

Delete Your Glow Up Diary Account

Glow Up Diary, by Abdul Ahad (Undercover Mysteries)

You can permanently delete your Glow Up Diary account and all associated data directly from within the app, in under a minute. No need to contact support to do this — it's fully self-serve.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the Glow Up Diary app and sign in to your account.
  2. Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm by tapping Delete forever in the dialog that appears.
This action is immediate and permanent. Once confirmed, your account cannot be recovered or restored.

What gets deleted

DataWhat happens
Account record (email, name, profile)Permanently deleted from our database immediately
Selfie metadata (dates, day numbers)Permanently deleted from our database immediately
Selfie photo filesDeleted from your device immediately — these were never stored on our servers in the first place

There is no retention period — none of this data is kept after deletion. The only exception is anything Apple or Google retain on their own end related to your subscription/purchase history through the App Store or Play Store billing systems, which we don't control and which follow their own retention policies.
